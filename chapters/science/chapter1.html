Soil Preparation

1. Ploughing

Loosening soil using traditional ploughs or modern cultivators.

Purpose: Aeration, weed removal, and mixing nutrients.

2. Levelling

Using wooden/iron levellers for an even field surface.

Purpose: Ensures uniform water distribution during irrigation.

3. Manuring

Adding organic/nutrient supplements to improve soil fertility.

Types: Farmyard manure, compost, and green manure.

Quick Quiz

Q1. Why does paddy need standing water?

Paddy roots require anaerobic conditions, and standing water suppresses weeds.

Q2. What is the difference between Kharif and Rabi crops?

Kharif crops are grown during the monsoon season (June-September), while Rabi crops are grown during the winter season (October-March).
